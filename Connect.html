About Shutterfly Connect

Shutterfly Connect is our initiative to bridge creative communities. We partner with societies, clubs, and organizations to co-create compelling media, from event photography and videography to promotional campaigns and documentaries. Our goal is to amplify your story, providing professional media coverage and artistic vision to help your events and projects shine.

How It Works

1. Pitch Your Idea

Reach out to us with your project idea, event details, and media needs. The more information, the better!

2. We Plan & Prepare

Our team will meet with you to understand your vision, discuss logistics, and create a custom media plan tailored to your needs.

3. Let's Create!

Our crew will execute the plan, capturing your event and producing high-quality media content you'll be proud to share.
