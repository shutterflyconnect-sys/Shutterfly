Our Mission

The Student Culture team is dedicated to designing and implementing creative activities that engage students in a fun and meaningful way. We believe that college is about more than just academics; it's about building a community and creating lasting memories. By organizing events tailored to your interests, we aim to enhance participation and ignite enthusiasm among students for being actively involved in campus life. Our vision is to make every day a vibrant experience.

Our Past Events

Red Flag Green Flag

On February 7, 2025, the Student Culture Team of Shutterfly held a Pre-Valentine Event with two activities—"Red Flag, Green Flag," a discussion on modern relationships, and "Talk Our Way," a prank-based challenge. The lively event was marked by laughter, enthusiasm, and teamwork.

Valentines Day

On 14th February 2025, the Student Culture Team of Shutterfly hosted a Valentine’s-themed event featuring fun pair-based games. Activities included a three-legged race, Dumb Charades with sound-blocking headphones, a water overflow challenge, and a newspaper dance where the space shrank as music played.

Suggestion Board

The Student Culture Team of Shutterfly set up an “Expectation Board” where freshers shared their ideas and wishes for college life on sticky notes, helping SCT gather insights to plan future events and activities that match student interests.
